General

This course is your go to for anime drawing and illustration and design making. You will learn everything from drawing the body, to learning digital painting and compositions!

This course will take you from having little to no knowledge in anime drawing and painting, to having a much better foundation so that you can build and customize your own artwork at a much more advanced level.

INICIO 19 ENERO, 2022
FINALIZA 22 FEBRERO, 2022
MATRÍCULAS 6 DICIEMBRE, 2021 - 14 ENERO, 2022
MODALIDAD VIRTUAL
DURACIÓN 64 HORAS

Requisitos:

Técnicos

  • Basic photoshop knowledge (or any kind of program you are comfortable using)
  • Drawing tablet (wacom, huicon, iPad pro, etc) but pencil and paper is ok too

Dirigido a:

  • Beginner artists wanting to draw anime
  • Beginner artists wanting to learn basic anatomy
  • Artists who want to learn how to design characters
  • Artists who want to learn how to make illustrations

Certificación:

Se entregará el certificado digital, cuando el estudiante haya aprobado el curso con un mínimo de 70/100 puntos.
